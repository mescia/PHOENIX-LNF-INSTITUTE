Short-Stay Visitors (1–4 weeks)

Targeted visits for joint papers, code/data sprints, or rapid exploration of new directions in phenomenology aligned with LNF experimental activities.

  • Office and guesthouse accommodation (according to availability)
  • Whiteboard days & focused working sessions
  • Open seminars and discussion forums
  • Ideal for kick-starting new collaborations

How to Apply

Eligibility

  • Researchers in phenomenology and related theory areas
  • Clear synergy with LNF’s experimental activities
  • Commitment to participate in PHOENIX seminars/discussions

What to Prepare

  • CV and publication list (PDF)
  • 1–2 page statement of purpose (project & LNF synergy)
  • Preferred dates (1–4 weeks), desired collaborators

Support

  • Office and guesthouse accommodation — according to availability
  • Seminar logistics and collaboration matchmaking
